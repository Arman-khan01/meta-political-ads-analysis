📊 Unit III: Exploratory Visualizations
Objective 1: Demographic Profiling

The Question: Which specific demographic groups are targeted the most by Indian political advertisements?

The Distinct Columns: top_targeted_age and top_targeted_gender (Categorical Data)

Objective 2: Cost-to-Reach Efficiency

The Question: Is there a direct mathematical correlation between the financial budget of an ad and its actual reach?

The Distinct Columns: avg_spend and avg_impressions (Continuous Financial Data)

Objective 3: Platform Content Strategy

The Question: Do advertisers write longer, more complex ad copy for Facebook compared to Instagram?

The Distinct Columns: ad_text_length, on_facebook, and on_instagram (NLP/Text Data & Boolean Variables)

Objective 4: Temporal Campaign Trends

The Question: Who are the top political advertisers, and what do their daily campaign launch timelines look like?

The Distinct Columns: page_name and ad_delivery_start_time (Text & Time-Series Datetime Data)
